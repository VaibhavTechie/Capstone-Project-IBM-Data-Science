Color-Labeled Launch Outcomes Map

This map displays the launch sites with color-labeled markers indicating the outcomes of various launches. Each color represents a different outcome, providing a visual overview of the success rates and failure points.

Key Elements:

The map highlights the following details:

  • Green Markers: Successful landings.
  • Red Markers: Failed landings.
  • Other Colors: Indicate various outcomes such as no attempts or controlled landings.

Observations:

  • The map shows a concentration of launches at key sites.
  • Visual differentiation of successful and unsuccessful landings provides clear insights into the performance over time.

Conclusion:

Utilizing color-labeled markers on the map allows for quick and effective analysis of launch outcomes, helping to identify patterns and areas for improvement.
