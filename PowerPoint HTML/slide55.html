Conclusions

  • Data Collection and Preparation:
    • Successfully collected data from various sources, including launch records, economic indicators, and other relevant variables.
    • Data preprocessing involved handling missing values, normalization, and feature selection to ensure the models received clean and relevant input.
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):
    • Visualized key trends and patterns in the data, such as the correlation between payload mass and launch success, and the distribution of launch outcomes across different sites.
    • Identified significant features contributing to launch success, such as booster version, payload mass, and launch site.
  • Model Performance:
    • Developed and evaluated multiple machine learning models, including Logistic Regression, SVM, Decision Tree, and KNN.
    • Decision Tree achieved the highest accuracy of 87.5%, followed by SVM and KNN with 84.82%, and Logistic Regression with 84.64%.
    • Confusion matrices for all models indicated consistent performance, with a similar number of true positives and true negatives.
  • Insights from Maps and Plots:
    • Mapped global launch sites, highlighting strategic locations and their access to various orbital trajectories.
    • Visualizations indicated that most launch sites are located along coasts to facilitate easier access to orbital paths.
  • Key Findings:
    • The Decision Tree model outperformed others in predicting launch success, suggesting its suitability for this type of classification task.
    • All models were more effective at predicting successful landings compared to predicting failures, indicating a potential area for improvement.
    • Visualization tools such as Folium and plotly provided valuable insights into geographical and statistical aspects of the data.
  • Future Work:
    • Focus on improving the models' ability to predict failures by refining feature selection and exploring additional machine learning techniques.
    • Continue updating the dataset with new launch records to maintain model accuracy and relevance.
    • Investigate the impact of additional features, such as weather conditions and launch timing, on model performance.
