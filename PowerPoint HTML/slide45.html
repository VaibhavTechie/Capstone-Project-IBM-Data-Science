Launch Success Count Dashboard

This dashboard displays the total success launches count for all SpaceX launch sites in a pie chart format. Each section of the pie chart represents the proportion of successful launches for a specific launch site.

Key Elements:

The dashboard includes the following components:

  • Total Success Launches By Site: A pie chart showing the distribution of successful launches across different launch sites.
  • Payload Range (Kg): A slider to filter the launches based on the payload mass.
  • Correlation between Payload and Success: A scatter plot displaying the correlation between the payload mass and the success of the launches for all sites.

Observations:

  • The majority of successful launches are from CCAFS LC-40, followed by KSC LC-39A and VAFB SLC-4E.
  • The payload range slider allows for interactive filtering of the data to analyze specific payload ranges.
  • The scatter plot indicates the relationship between payload mass and the success of the launches, with different colors representing various booster versions.

Conclusion:

This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of the success rates of SpaceX launches across different sites, allowing for detailed analysis of payload impacts and site-specific performance. The visualizations help in identifying key patterns and insights into SpaceX's launch history.
