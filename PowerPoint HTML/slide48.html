Correlation between Payload and Success for VAFB SLC-4E

This scatter plot shows the correlation between payload mass and launch success outcomes for the VAFB SLC-4E launch site. The data points are color-coded based on the booster version category.

Key Elements:

  • Payload Range: The x-axis represents the payload mass in kilograms.
  • Success Class: The y-axis represents the success class (0 for failure and 1 for success).
  • Booster Versions: Different colors represent different booster version categories (v1.1, FT).

Observations:

  • There are fewer data points for the VAFB SLC-4E site, indicating fewer launches compared to other sites.
  • Successful launches (class 1) are observed in lower payload ranges (up to 1000 kg).
  • Failures (class 0) are also present but do not show a distinct pattern.

Conclusion:

The scatter plot suggests that for the VAFB SLC-4E launch site, successful launches are more common in lower payload ranges. However, the limited number of data points makes it challenging to draw a definitive conclusion regarding the correlation between payload mass and launch success.
